About us

Established in 1990 the cocktail lounge began as a small family run business. It offered and introduced the local community to a wide range of new and exciting cocktail mixes. Today it is a popular and enigmatic destination. With professional cocktail bar staff and new, exciting recipes the expanding and delicious menu is unmatched.

The menu has the perfect combination of new and old classic cocktails with many non-alcoholic options available. Our venue is perfect setting for intermate gathering, celebrations and large parties. We are also available for private hire just contact us for more information.

For a great evening out come and join us at the cocktail lounge.
